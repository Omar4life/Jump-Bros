Jump Bros is a retro Nintendo-inspired local multiplayer platformer with pixelated 8-bit graphics.

Gameplay:
Two players compete to collect all coins and defeat all enemies. Getting touched by an enemy respawns you at the starting position. Score points by collecting coins (100 pts) and defeating enemies (200 pts).

Controls:
Player 1: WASD (W to jump)
Player 2: Arrow Keys (Up to jump)
R: Restart game
Features:
Classic pixel art style with Mario-inspired characters
Platform jumping with gravity mechanics
Collectible coins and enemy encounters
Local multiplayer competition
Nintendo-style retro visuals and sounds
